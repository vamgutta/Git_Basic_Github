[APP-NAME] [INFO|ERROR|WARNING] (TIME-STAMP | dim) Message

[catalogue]

RED, GREEN , YELLOW, BLUE, MAGENTA , CYAN

BLUE      - MONGODB, MYSQLDB, RABBITMQ, REDIS
MAGENTA   - WEB
CYAN      - CATALOGUE, CART, PAYMENT, SHIPPING, DISPATCH, USER
